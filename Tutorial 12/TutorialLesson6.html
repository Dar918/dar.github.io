Introduction to Hogwarts Legacy Characters



Sebastian Sallow is a charming and rebellious Slytherin with a talent for dueling and a curiosity for forbidden magic. He’s determined to find a cure for his twin sister’s mysterious illness, even if it means turning to the Dark Arts. Bold and stubborn, he often clashes with authority but means well deep down. His choices can lead to serious consequences.








Ominis Gaunt is a soft-spoken and serious Slytherin from a powerful pure-blood family with a dark legacy. Though blind, he uses magic to navigate the world with confidence. He’s deeply against the Dark Arts and often acts as the voice of reason among his friends. Ominis is loyal, thoughtful, and burdened by his family’s past.








Imelda Reyes is a fierce and competitive Slytherin who takes Quidditch more seriously than almost anything else. She’s confident in her flying skills and doesn’t hesitate to challenge others to prove her point. While she can seem harsh or arrogant, she has a strong sense of pride and ambition. Imelda pushes others to be better, even if it annoys them.













Natsai Onai, or Natty, is a courageous Gryffindor who transferred from the magical school Uagadou in Africa. She’s wise, compassionate, and deeply values justice. Natty often steps in when others are in trouble and stays calm under pressure. Her sense of right and wrong guides her choices, even in dangerous situations.









Garreth Weasley is an adventurous Gryffindor known for his wild potion experiments and endless curiosity. A cousin of the famous Weasley family, he’s always trying to invent something new, sometimes with explosive results. Garreth’s enthusiasm can get him into trouble, but his heart is in the right place. He brings humor and chaos to any situation.









Leander Prewett is a determined Gryffindor who wants to prove he’s brave and capable, even if it means being overly competitive. He often challenges others and has a bit of a temper when things don’t go his way. Though he can come off as arrogant, he’s just trying to earn respect. Underneath, he’s more insecure than he lets on.













Amit Thakkar is a clever and curious Ravenclaw with a huge passion for astronomy, books, and magical history. He dreams of writing famous wizarding guides and often talks about facts others weren’t even asking for. Though he’s not the most confident in battle, he’s always eager to help. Amit shines brightest when exploring or learning something new.









Everett Clompton is a mischievous Ravenclaw who loves playing pranks and bending the rules just enough to stay out of serious trouble. He’s clever, witty, and often jokes his way through tense moments. While not the most focused student, he’s surprisingly observant and quick-thinking. Everett keeps things interesting at Hogwarts—sometimes a little too interesting.









Samantha Dale is a gentle and thoughtful Ravenclaw who has a deep love for magical plants and nature. She’s curious, observant, and often spends her time in the greenhouses tending to rare herbs. Samantha has a calming presence and always tries to understand others before judging them. Her intelligence shines most when she’s learning through hands-on experience.













Poppy Sweeting is a quiet and kind Hufflepuff who feels more comfortable around magical beasts than people. She’s passionate about protecting creatures from harm, especially from poachers. Though she’s soft-spoken, she’s incredibly brave and willing to stand up for what’s right. Her bond with animals shows her gentle strength.









Sacharissa Tugwood is a proud and confident student with a keen interest in beauty potions and personal care. She’s known for her elegance and obsession with perfection, often going above and beyond to maintain her appearance. While she can come off as self-absorbed, she genuinely believes in the power of self-expression through magical cosmetics. Sacharissa is sharp, stylish, and always ready to give advice, whether you asked for it or not.









Arthur Plummly is an excitable and slightly awkward student with a big imagination and a love for magical history. He’s always chasing after rumors of hidden secrets or forgotten relics around Hogwarts. Though he tends to get lost, both literally and in his thoughts, his enthusiasm is contagious. Arthur’s a bit clumsy, but his heart is always in the right place.
